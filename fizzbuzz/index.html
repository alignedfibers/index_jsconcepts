Fizzbuzz challenge in JS Description
The Fizzbuz challenge is commonly imposed during an interview to assess the steps taken by a candidate to provide a solution to a problem. The exact challenge may vary, and progressively be made more difficult if a candidate successfully completes the first challenge. The challenge though varying, is essentially asking the candidate to print all numbers from {X - Z} supplementing the word "Fizz" for multiples of number Y, "Buzz" for multiples of W and FizzBuzz for numbers which are multiples of both Y and W. This may be made more difficult by varying the range, using negative numbers in the range, adding more multiples or other control variables. Asking the candidate to modify the code written in challenge one will help assess the process taken to make changes to the existing code.

The example below demonstrates printing numbers 1-100, supplementing "Fizz" for multiples of 3, "Buzz" for multiples of 5 and FizzBuzz for multiples of 3 and 5.
